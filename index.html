Biography —

I am a 23 year-old audio engineer and recording artist located in the Greater Twin Cities, and an establishing member of theMARAJ LLC music collective. Genres that I focus on are R&B, hip-hop, rap, EDM, dancehall, pop, funk, soul, alternative, and reggaeton. I specialize in writing choruses and original melodies, and I have featured on tracks with rising artists on a national and local level.

I have over eight years of experience using Logic Pro X, which I primarily use for recording, editing, and mixing. I am also a seasoned social media manager and photographer, with three years of experience using the Adobe Creative Suite. I will be graduating from the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities in 2022 with a Bachelor of Arts in Cinema & Media Culture, and a minor in Digital Media.
